model repo ↗ methodology ↗

Screening model, honestly labeled: every value is an aggregate tract-level indicator — never a prediction about individuals. *The pressure surface is a proxy composite of literature-based correlates; observed homelessness counts exist only at Continuum-of-Care level. The 2030 surface is a labeled persistence extrapolation, not a forecast; development probability broadcasts county permits to tracts (stated in the methodology). Indices computed from ACS 2019–2023 & 2009–2013, LEHD LODES 2013–2022, Census BPS permits, CDC/ATSDR SVI 2022, MassGIS (MBTA, trains, EJ 2020), and HUD PIT validation by the open pipeline in predicting-housing-crisis.
