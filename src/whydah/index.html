Evidence has a confidence level.

The surviving record is incomplete, and pirate stories are unusually good at turning repetition into “fact.” The project labels major claims as documented, contested, or mythologized and explains why.

It also treats the Whydah’s history honestly: before pirates captured it, the ship was built for the transatlantic slave trade. That history is part of the story, not a footnote.
