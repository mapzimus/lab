About.

Mapzimus is the creative lab of Maxwell Howe — GIS professional by trade, maker of browser tools, unusual maps, and small games by compulsion.

If you found this site instead of the polished portfolio: welcome, this is the fun one. Mapzimus is where everything lives — every tool, every map, every game, every experiment, whether it behaves like a portfolio piece or not.

The curated professional GIS work is at maxwellhowegis.com. The lab's process notes are in Field Notes, and everything I've built is browsable from the sections above.
