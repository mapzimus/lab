About

The person behind the lab

Mapzimus is the personal lab of Maxwell Howe — GIS analyst and math teacher on Massachusetts' North Shore.

This is the fun half of my work. I build small browser tools I actually use — coordinate converters, geocoders, chart makers, classroom helpers — along with maps, games, and experiments that don't need to justify themselves. Everything here is free, runs in your browser, and asks for nothing.

The serious half lives at maxwellhowegis.com: case studies, professional GIS work, and the projects I'd show an employer. If you're deciding whether to hire me, start there. If you just want a good sun calculator, stay here.

Notes on what I'm building are in Field Notes, and the radars track what's new in GIS and dev tooling every day.
